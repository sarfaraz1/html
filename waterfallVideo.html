Wonder of the World

This is a video of victoria waterfall. It lies on the Zimbezi river which is between the border of Zambia and Zimbabwe. The Victoria fall can be seen from 25 to 40 miles away. The roaring of the water can be heard from a long distance. The native people call it as "mosi-oa-tunya" ,that means "smoke that thunders". The falls were named in honor of Queen Victoria.
